Week 3

You’ve defeated the Calamari! Oh, thank goodness, I was a bit worried for a moment there but it all worked out.

As it so happens, we’ve also landed right where we needed to! We’re now on Shitcoin Shores, and we’re one step closer to getting back to The Land Of Magical Internet Money.

You’ve picked up a lot of knowledge along the way and now have a good grasp of blockchain and cryptocurrency, and even how to acquire cryptocurrency and store it in a wallet! Now, let’s learn about a few ways to use your cryptocurrency.

You can play games! More and more cryptocurrency-based games are being developed and include unique concepts like NFTs, which could very well be the future of gaming.

You can earn more crypto! One of the biggest movements in Ethereum is with Decentralized Finance, or #DeFi, which is growing in popularity and allows you to do things such as gain interest on your cryptocurrency.

You can share your crypto with the world! One of the best things you can do with your cryptocurrency is give some to another, whether it’s through charity or a tipping service, or just sending some to a friend to get them started on their journey.

Ah, there’s the entrance to the forest! It’s full of interesting creatures and foliage but I’ve a feeling that the worst is behind us.

Okay, we’re almost into the forest. We should be able to pass through with relative ease, but just in case, here are a few pieces of equipment that may be beneficial to add to your arsenal.

The first is the Pirate Jacket. To receive this, head over to GiveCrypto, a nonprofit that distributes cryptocurrency to people living in poverty.

Next, there’s the Scuba Suit. For this item, you’ll need to visit Compound Finance, where you can start gaining interest on your cryptocurrency!

The last option is the Water Wings. To get this item, check out the Gods Unchained game and redeem your first free pack of cards! Note: Your free cards will be given once create an account and initiate the game download.
