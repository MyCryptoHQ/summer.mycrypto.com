Welcome to MyCryptoSummer!

Welcome to MyCryptoSummer! Throughout the month of August, we want to help you learn more about blockchain and cryptocurrency - but in a fun, digestible manner.

How does it work? It’s simple: Each week, a new portion of the adventure unlocks. Experience the story, choose your path, and at the end of the month, you'll have a fully equipped adventurer!

Make sure to share your progress each week on Twitter using #MyCryptoSummer - you’ll be entered to win prizes! For full details, visit the announcement post.
