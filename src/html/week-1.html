Hey, you. You’re finally awake. Sorry for the confusion, but you’re the only one that can help!

I desperately need to get back to The Land Of Magical Internet Money, and thankfully you showed up.

What is The Land Of Magical Internet Money? Oh, I’ll tell you all about it.

In The Land Of Magical Internet Money, we’ve developed blockchain technology, and as a result, cryptocurrency!

Our blockchain is an efficient, transparent method of digitally storing transaction records. The data within our records cannot be modified or deleted, it can only be added to. It’s also not governed by any single body, which makes it decentralized.

Cryptocurrency is a token platform built on top of the blockchain. These tokens are the basis for sending transactions on the blockchain - some of these tokens carry value and some carry information. The benefits of utilizing this technology for our people are far-reaching. We’re able to transact with those all across our land, regardless of their location, and without unnecessary fees. Transactions are also nearly instantaneous.

And this is just the start! As we continue to use and develop this technology, we discover more and more useful scenarios.

Alright, that’s enough for now. We have a serious task at hand.

So, will you help me get back to The Land Of Magical Internet Money? If so, I have a few items which may assist you on our journey.

First is the Pirate Hat, obtained by Downloading the Brave Browser, which is a secure, private, fast browser and a great companion for anyone—especially those traversing the world of blockchain!

The second is the Snorkel! Head over to Coinbase’s Earn page. This will walk you through important concepts and get you some free crypto for your efforts.

Lastly is the Umbrella Hat. Explore the EthHub documentation to learn everything you need to know about the Ethereum blockchain.

Take whichever one you want. Get some rest and we’ll set sail soon!
